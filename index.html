What is Paragliding

The short answer is paragliding an adventure sport using a modified parachute. The lightweight, inflatable wing, is motorless and can be foot-launched with the opportunity to glide or climb upwards. It's a sport that combines the thrills of flying with the peacefulness of soaring through the sky. It's a great way to experience nature, and it can be enjoyed by people of all ages and abilities.

Paragliding is a form of flying that uses a special parachute-like wing. The wing is designed to catch air and lift the pilot off the ground. This makes it possible for pilots to fly very low to the ground, or even mountain tops.

To paraglide, you'll need some basic equipment. You'll need a paragliding wing, a harness, a helmet, and some training. You can find all of this equipment and more at a local paragliding shop.
